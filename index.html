ALaughingHorse

Hi there! Welcome and nice to meet you virtually. You may wonder why it is website called "A Laughing Horse" with a human-being's picture underneath - how strange!

This is because my Chinese name is "Ma Xiao", which literally means "Horse Laugh (Roar)" in English and when it came to making a made-up name, I decided to go with "ALaughingHorse" - a funny but accurate epitome of both my name and my personality. (Interestingly, I chose my English name to be Marshal when I came to the U.S., which essentially pronounce the same as my real name.)

Graduated from UC Berkeley with a statistics major, I am now working as a data scientist. In my free time, I enjoy reading (and gaming) a lot, and I would also write some of my ideas into blogs and share it with people. Here, you will find the blogs I wrote - mostly are and will be about data science; the programming packages I built - mostly for data science, and the books I read/reading - mostly have nothing to do with data science. I hope you find it easy enought to navigate!
